Built Different

The details that make
it actually useful

Every feature was added because it solved a real friction point — nothing is here for show.

🎵
QC/Delivery Checklist
Click the final step segment and a full 11-item production checklist opens — Scratch & Structure through to ISRC export. Archive is locked until every item is checked.
⊙
Focus Mode
One click removes every distraction. Full-screen view with a sidebar track list, large step segments, notes, lyrics, and BPM/Key meta — everything you need, nothing you don't.
🔍
Global Search
Press / anywhere to search across all tracks, projects, notes, books, and archived months. Filter by category. Results navigate directly to the right tab.
✦
Hall of Fame
Completed projects get a gold vinyl treatment with liner notes, completion date, and days-to-master. A permanent record of everything you've shipped.
📅
Google Calendar Integration
Connect your Google Calendar to see upcoming events in a 7-day bar. Drag any event onto a track to set it as a deadline. Badges turn red as the date approaches.
📱
Mobile-First on Android
Swipe between tabs. Bottom navigation bar. FAB + action sheet for quick adds. 44px tap targets on every card action. Designed for one-handed use on your phone.
🔄
Monthly Carry-Over
At month end, choose what carries forward. Each unfinished item gets Carry Over, Archive As-Is, or Drop. Monthly snapshots preserved forever in History.
🎉
Celebration Animations
A spinning vinyl with particle burst plays when a track hits 100%. A larger gold celebration fires when you archive to the Hall of Fame. Earned, not cheap.
🔒
Secure Sync
Login once. Your full workspace syncs in real time across desktop and Android. Passwords hashed with bcrypt. JWT auth. Data stored on Railway PostgreSQL.
Music Production Workflow

From first scratch to
distributor upload

The 11-step mastering meter maps your exact production process. Each segment is clickable, renameable, and colour-coded by stage.

01–03
Tracking
Pre-Production, Guitar tracking, Bass tracking — establish the foundation. Scratch tracks with BPM and click.
04–07
Editing & Vocals
Drum edit, Guitar edit, Vocal tracking, Comping — tighten the performances, record and comp vocals.
08–10
Mix & Master
Mixing, Master Prep, Mastering — balance, tone, dynamics, and final loudness for streaming.
11
QC / Delivery
Click this segment to open the full QC checklist — ISRC, WAV export, distributor upload. Every box must be checked before the track archives to the Hall of Fame.
